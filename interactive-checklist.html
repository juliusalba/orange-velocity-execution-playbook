Month 1: Build Your Foundation

Establish processes, hire your team, and get your first 3-5 clients

Month 1

Foundation Building

Set Up Legal & Branding
Register business, get logo, build simple website
Setup checklist:
  1. Business registration: Sole proprietor or LLC
  2. Logo & branding: Canva or hire designer
  3. Simple website: Landing page + services
  4. Email domain: Custom email address
  5. Timeline: 1-2 weeks
Document Your Process
Create SOPs for ad management, reporting, client communication
Key SOPs to document:
  1. Campaign setup: Facebook, Google Ads, TikTok
  2. Daily/Weekly reporting: KPIs, dashboards
  3. Client communication: Calls, updates, feedback
  4. Optimization process: A/B testing, scaling rules
  5. Tool: Notion, Google Docs, or Loom videos
Hire Your First Specialist
1 full-time creative or junior ads manager
Hiring guide:
  1. Role: Junior ads manager or creative specialist
  2. Salary: PHP 25-30k/month
  3. Responsibilities: Ad creation, basic optimization, support
  4. Hiring channels: LinkedIn, job boards, referrals
  5. Training: 2-3 weeks on your process
Close First 3-5 Clients
Your "proof of concept" - get your first wins
Sales strategy:
  1. Target: Local e-commerce or SaaS businesses
  2. Package: Starter tier (PHP 15-20k/month)
  3. Pitch: "Proven system, dedicated team, guaranteed growth"
  4. Goal: Month 1: 3-5 clients | MRR: PHP 45-100k
  5. Demo: Show case studies, social proof

Month 3

Outreach & Authority

Launch LinkedIn Strategy
Post 3x/week, build network, establish authority

Blog posts, case studies, video series
Content calendar:
  1. Blog: 2x/month (SEO-optimized)

Month 4

Scaling & Team Growth

  5. Goal: Land 2-3 enterprise clients
