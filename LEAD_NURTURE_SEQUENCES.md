# Lead Nurturing Sequences & Outreach Templates

**Date:** November 2025
**Purpose:** Automated sequences to convert leads to clients
**Goal:** 40% of leads convert through nurture sequences

---

## Overview: Nurture Strategy

**Why Nurture Sequences Matter:**
- 80% of leads aren't ready to buy immediately
- Nurtured leads make 47% larger purchases
- Nurture sequences can automate 70% of follow-up

**Our Approach:**
- **Segment by ICP** (different messages for different people)
- **Segment by Lead Score** (different cadence for hot vs warm leads)
- **Value-first** (educate, don't just sell)
- **Track engagement** (open rates, click rates, replies)

# SEGMENTATION & AUTOMATION

## Lead Scoring Triggers

### Automatic Segmentation

**Hot Lead (81-100 points):**
- Downloaded pricing guide
- Attended webinar
- Requested case study
- Filled out "Book a Call" form
- Replied to email with questions

**Warm Lead (61-80 points):**
- Opened 3+ emails
- Clicked links 2+ times
- Visited pricing page
- Watched video testimonials
- Downloaded one resource

**Cold Lead (41-60 points):**
- Opened 1-2 emails
- Visited website once
- Downloaded nothing
- No engagement

### Automation Rules

**Hot Lead Triggers:**
```
IF Lead Score >= 81
THEN:
- Add to "Hot Leads" sequence
- Send notification to sales team (Slack)
- Create task for sales rep (follow up within 24 hours)
- Add to "Priority" list in CRM
```

**Warm Lead Triggers:**
```
IF Lead Score 61-80
THEN:
- Add to "Warm Leads" sequence
- Add to weekly nurture email
- Track engagement
- Follow up weekly (manual)
```

**Cold Lead Triggers:**
```
IF Lead Score 41-60
THEN:
- Add to "Cold Leads" sequence
- Monthly check-in
- Remove from active nurture after 90 days
```

# A/B TESTING

## Test Subject Lines

### High-Performing Patterns
1. **Question + Benefit**
   - "Quick question about [Company]'s ads"
   - "Is CAC a challenge for your business?"

2. **Curiosity + Specificity**
   - "How [Brand] reduced CAC by 53%"
   - "The biggest e-commerce ad mistake"

3. **Value + Personalization**
   - "Thought you'd find this interesting, [First Name]"
   - "Quick insight for [Company]"

### A/B Testing Schedule
- **Test 1:** Subject line (send 50% each variation)
- **Test 2:** Email length (short vs long)
- **Test 3:** Call-to-action (direct vs soft)
- **Test 4:** Send time (morning vs afternoon)
- **Test 5:** Day of week (Tuesday vs Thursday)

## Test Results Documentation

**Track for Each Test:**
- Variation A: Subject line
- Variation B: Subject line
- Send count (A vs B)
- Open rate (A vs B)
- Click rate (A vs B)
- Winner: [Variance]
- Next action: [Implement winner]
