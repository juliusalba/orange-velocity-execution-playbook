## Target Market Segments

### Segment 1: E-commerce Businesses
**Market Size:** ~25,000 active e-commerce stores
**Annual Revenue:** PHP 5M - PHP 500M
**Ad Spend:** PHP 100k - PHP 2M monthly

#### Characteristics:
- **Business Model:** B2C product sales (fashion, beauty, home, electronics)
- **Platform:** Mostly Facebook/Instagram, some Shopee/Lazada
- **Pain Points:**
  - High customer acquisition costs
  - Poor conversion rates (1-3% average)

  - Lack of data-driven optimization

## Timing Considerations

### Peak Seasons (Higher Budgets)
1. **Q4 (October-December):**
   - Christmas shopping surge
   - 11.11, 12.12 sales events
   - **Strategy:** Pitch early (August) for Q4 prep

2. **Q1 (January-March):**
   - New Year fresh start budget
   - Chinese New Year campaigns
   - **Strategy:** Offer "New Year New Results" packages

3. **Mid-Year (June-July):**
   - Mid-year performance reviews
   - Budget available for growth
   - **Strategy:** "Mid-year growth" packages

### Low Seasons (Focus on Retention)
- **February:** Post-holiday slowdown
- **September:** Before Q4 rush
- **Strategy:** Focus on existing clients, improve systems

## Risk Factors

### Market Risks
1. **Economic Downturn** - Businesses cut marketing budgets
2. **Platform Changes** - iOS updates, algorithm changes
3. **New Competitors** - Large agencies entering market
4. **Client Churn** - High churn in SMB segment

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Diversify Client Base** - Not too dependent on one industry
2. **Platform Diversification** - Facebook, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn
3. **Retention Focus** - Build long-term relationships
4. **Value-Add Services** - Not just ad management
