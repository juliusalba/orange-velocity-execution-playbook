# Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP): Detailed Targeting Guide

**Date:** November 2025
**Purpose:** Precise targeting for Orange Velocity
**Target:** PHP 500k MRR in 6 months

---

## Overview: 4 Primary ICPs

Based on market research, we've identified 4 distinct customer profiles that offer the best fit for Orange Velocity's services:

1. **E-commerce Growth-Stage** (Primary ICP - 50% of effort)
2. **SaaS Scale-Up** (Secondary ICP - 25% of effort)
3. **Traditional Business Going Digital** (Tertiary ICP - 15% of effort)
4. **Digital Agencies (White-Label)** (Partnership - 10% of effort)

Each ICP has unique characteristics, pain points, and buying behaviors. This guide provides detailed targeting information for each.

---

# ICP #1: E-commerce Growth-Stage

## Demographics

### Company Profile
- **Annual Revenue:** PHP 20M - PHP 200M
- **Monthly Ad Spend:** PHP 100k - PHP 800k
- **Team Size:** 10-50 employees
- **Years in Business:** 2-7 years
- **Growth Stage:** Early growth to scale-up

### Business Model
- **Industry:** Fashion, Beauty, Home & Living, Electronics, Health & Wellness
- **Platform:** Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom e-commerce platform
- **Primary Channel:** Facebook/Instagram Ads (70%), Google Ads (20%), TikTok (10%)
- **Product Range:** 50-500 SKUs

### Decision Makers

#### Primary Decision Maker: Founder/CEO
- **Age:** 28-45 years old
- **Education:** College degree (Business, Marketing, or related)
- **Background:**
  - Serial entrepreneur or first-time founder
  - Built business from 0 to current revenue
  - Hands-on with marketing decisions
  - Data-driven mindset

- **Personality Traits:**
  - Ambitious growth-oriented
  - Results-focused
  - Willing to invest in growth
  - Skeptical but open to proven solutions
  - Values efficiency and ROI

#### Secondary Decision Maker: Marketing Manager/E-commerce Manager
- **Age:** 25-40 years old
- **Education:** Marketing, Business, or Communications degree
- **Background:**
  - 3-7 years marketing experience
  - Some digital marketing knowledge
  - Reports to founder/CEO
  - Often handles day-to-day execution

- **Personality Traits:**
  - Detail-oriented
  - Chasing results
  - Frustrated with current performance
  - Wants to look good to founder
  - Values tools and processes

### Pain Points & Challenges

#### Primary Pain Points
1. **Rising CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**
   - Current CAC: PHP 200-500
   - Target CAC: <PHP 300
   - Impact: Reduced profit margins, scaling challenges

2. **Inconsistent Ad Performance**
   - Good weeks followed by terrible weeks
   - No systematic testing approach
   - Ad fatigue after 2-3 weeks

3. **Poor Conversion Rates**
   - Current: 1-3% conversion rate
   - Industry benchmark: 2-4%
   - Lost revenue from poor landing pages/funnels

4. **Scaling Challenges**
   - Can't scale past current ad spend
   - Running out of audiences
   - Creative fatigue

#### Secondary Pain Points
- **Lack of Time:** Founder juggling everything
- **No Data-Driven Decisions:** Guessing instead of testing
- **Poor ROI Tracking:** Not sure what's working
- **Platform Changes:** Confused by iOS updates, algorithm changes

### Goals & Desired Outcomes

#### Business Goals
- **Revenue Growth:** 50-200% YoY growth
- **Market Expansion:** Enter new markets (SEA)
- **Profitability:** Improve profit margins
- **Exit Readiness:** Build for acquisition or IPO

#### Marketing Goals
- **Reduce CAC:** By 20-30%
- **Increase Conversion Rate:** From 2% to 4%+
- **Scale Ad Spend:** From PHP 100k to PHP 500k/month
- **Improve ROAS:** From 2x to 4x+

### Current Marketing Setup

#### What's Working
- Some profitable campaigns
- Decent brand awareness
- Existing customer base

#### What's Not Working
- Inconsistent performance
- No systematic approach to testing
- Poor landing page optimization
- Limited creative assets
- No marketing automation

### Budget & Decision Timeline

#### Budget Allocation
- **Current Ad Spend:** PHP 100k-800k/month
- **Agency Fee Willingness:** 15-25% of ad spend
- **Budget Authority:** Founder approves (90% of cases)
- **Timeline:** Decision within 14-21 days

#### Investment Capacity
- **Starter Package:** PHP 15-20k/month
- **Growth Package:** PHP 25-35k/month
- **Enterprise Package:** PHP 50-75k/month
- **Most Likely:** Growth Package (PHP 25-35k)

### Where to Find Them

#### Online Communities
1. **Facebook Groups:**
   - Philippine E-commerce Entrepreneurs (50k+ members)
   - Shopee Sellers Philippines (100k+ members)
   - Online Sellers Philippines (75k+ members)
   - Fashion Business Philippines (25k+ members)
   - E-commerce Growth Hackers (10k+ members)

2. **LinkedIn:**
   - Search: "E-commerce" + "Founder" + "Philippines"
   - Groups: Philippine E-commerce Community
   - Hashtags: #ecommercePH #digitalmarketing #startupPH

3. **Websites:**
   - E-commerce summit Philippines
   - Digital Commerce Summit
   - GoNegosyo events

#### Direct Outreach
1. **Cold LinkedIn:**
   - Target: E-commerce founders in Manila/Cebu
   - Message: Value-first approach
   - Connection rate: 15-25%

2. **Email Outreach:**
   - Find email via LinkedIn or company website
   - Send case studies and value
   - Open rate: 25-40%

3. **Warm Introductions:**
   - Ask existing clients for referrals
   - Leverage university alumni networks
   - Attend local business events

### Buying Signals

#### High-Intent Signals
- Actively posting about growth challenges on LinkedIn
- Asking for ad agency recommendations in Facebook groups
- Attending e-commerce events and webinars
- Recently raised funding or experiencing rapid growth

#### Trigger Events
- **Growth Plateau:** Revenue stuck for 3+ months
- **New Funding:** Just raised capital
- **Competitor Success:** See competitors growing faster
- **Holiday Seasons:** Preparing for Q4/Christmas sales
- **Team Expansion:** Hired marketing manager

### Objections & Concerns

#### Common Objections
1. **"We tried an agency before and it didn't work"**
   - *Solution:* Focus on different approach, show specific case studies
   - *Positioning:* "We've learned from common mistakes..."

2. **"Agency fees are too expensive"**
   - *Solution:* Show ROI calculation, offer performance-based pricing
   - *Positioning:* "We're an investment, not an expense..."

3. **"We can do it ourselves"**
   - *Solution:* Acknowledge capability but highlight time/opportunity cost
   - *Positioning:* "Focus on what you do best..."

4. **"We need to think about it"**
   - *Solution:* Create urgency, limited spots, seasonal factors
   - *Positioning:* "Given Q4 is coming up..."

### What They Value

#### Top 5 Values
1. **Proven Results** (e.g., "We increased ROAS from 2.1x to 4.8x")
2. **Transparency** (real-time dashboards, weekly reports)
3. **Speed** (quick turnaround, fast iterations)
4. **Communication** (regular updates, easy to reach)
5. **Scalability** (can grow with them)

#### What They Don't Value
- Fancy presentations without substance
- Long-term contracts (want 3-month minimum)
- Lack of direct access to decision-makers
- Vague promises without specifics

### Winning Message Framework

#### Value Proposition
*"We help e-commerce brands scale from PHP 20M to PHP 200M+ revenue by reducing customer acquisition costs and increasing conversion rates through our proven 4-step system."*

#### Positioning Statements
- "The only ad agency that guarantees ROAS improvement within 60 days"
- "We turned a struggling fashion brand into a PHP 50M/year business"
- "Our clients grow 150% on average in their first year"

#### Call-to-Action
- "Book a free strategy call to audit your ads"
- "See how we increased ROAS by 240% for similar brands"
- "Get your custom growth plan (worth PHP 10k) for free"

# ICP #2: SaaS Scale-Up

## Demographics

### Company Profile
- **Annual Revenue:** PHP 50M - PHP 500M
- **Monthly Ad Spend:** PHP 150k - PHP 1M
- **Team Size:** 20-100 employees
- **Funding Stage:** Series A to Series C
- **Industry:** HR Tech, FinTech, EdTech, Productivity Tools, Marketing Tools

### Decision Makers

#### Primary Decision Maker: CMO/Head of Growth
- **Age:** 30-45 years old
- **Background:**
  - 7-15 years marketing experience
  - Previously worked at unicorn startups
  - Data-driven, analytical
  - Under pressure to hit growth targets

- **Challenges:**
  - High CAC in competitive markets
  - Long sales cycles (60-90 days)
  - Need to prove ROI to investors
  - Balancing growth vs. profitability

#### Secondary Decision Maker: CEO/Founder
- **Age:** 28-42 years old
- **Background:**
  - Technical or product background
  - Fundraising experience
  - Vision-driven
  - Final decision on agency partnerships

### Pain Points

#### Primary Pain Points
1. **High Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
   - CAC: PHP 5,000-15,000
   - Need to reduce by 30-50%
   - Long payback periods

2. **Long Sales Cycles**
   - 60-90 day average sales cycle
   - Need to nurture leads better
   - Low conversion rates (5-15%)

3. **Attribution Challenges**
   - Multiple touchpoints before conversion
   - Hard to track ROAS accurately
   - Need better funnel analytics

4. **Scaling Paid Acquisition**
   - Need consistent lead flow
   - Scaling = increasing CAC
   - Creative fatigue

### Goals
- Reduce CAC by 30-50%
- Increase conversion rate by 20-30%
- Scale from PHP 150k to PHP 1M+ ad spend
- Prove ROI to investors

### Budget
- **Willing to pay:** PHP 50-100k/month
- **Value long-term partnerships:** 12-24 month contracts
- **Price-sensitive but value quality**

### Where to Find Them
- LinkedIn (CMOs, Head of Growth)
- SaaS conferences (Tech in Asia, etc.)
- Founder Institute alumni
- Startup accelerators (IdeaSpace, etc.)

# ICP #3: Traditional Business Going Digital

## Demographics

### Company Profile
- **Annual Revenue:** PHP 20M - PHP 150M
- **Team Size:** 15-75 employees
- **Industry:** Restaurants, Retail, Professional Services, Healthcare, Real Estate
- **Years in Business:** 10-30 years
- **Digital Maturity:** Early stage (just starting online)

### Decision Makers

#### Primary Decision Maker: Business Owner
- **Age:** 35-60 years old
- **Background:**
  - Built business offline
  - Seeing decline in foot traffic
  - Forced to go online by COVID/market changes
  - Tech-resistant but motivated

### Pain Points
1. **No Digital Marketing Experience**
2. **Fear of Technology/Ads**
3. **Need Hand-Holding/Education**
4. **ROI-Focused (Quick Wins)**
5. **Price-Sensitive**

### Budget
- **Willing to pay:** PHP 10-20k/month
- **Need:** Education + results
- **Timeline:** Decision within 30-45 days

### Where to Find Them
- Chamber of Commerce events
- Local business networking
- Google: "brick and mortar" + "Philippines"
- Facebook groups by city/industry
