# Lead Qualification Framework & Scoring System

**Date:** November 2025
**Purpose:** Systematically qualify and prioritize leads
**Goal:** Increase close rate from 20% to 60%

---

## Overview

This framework helps you **quickly identify** which leads are worth pursuing and which should be deprioritized. Every lead gets scored within 5 minutes of first contact.

**Scoring Formula:**
- **0-40 points:** Disqualify (not a fit)
- **41-60 points:** Nurture (may become a fit later)
- **61-80 points:** Qualify (pursue actively)
- **81-100 points:** Priority (immediate follow-up)

---

## Qualification Framework (BANT + Pain + Fit)

### BANT Framework
1. **Budget** - Can they afford your services?
2. **Authority** - Are you talking to a decision-maker?
3. **Need** - Do they have a problem you can solve?
4. **Timeline** - When do they need a solution?

### Additional Factors
5. **Fit** - Do they match your ideal customer profile?
6. **Pain Level** - How urgent is their problem?
7. **Trigger Event** - What prompted them to reach out now?

## Qualification Call Script

### Discovery Call Framework (30 minutes)

#### Opening (2 minutes)
```
"Hey [Name], thanks for taking the time to speak with me.
Before we dive in, I want to make sure this is a good fit for both of us.

I work exclusively with e-commerce and SaaS businesses spending
PHP 100k+ monthly on ads who are serious about growth.

Does that sound like your situation?"
```

**IF YES:** Continue
**IF NO:** Politely disqualify and offer resource

#### Understanding Their Business (8 minutes)
```
1. "Tell me about your business - what do you sell and to whom?"
2. "How long have you been running ads?"
3. "What's your current monthly ad spend?"
4. "Who handles your marketing internally?"
5. "What results are you seeing right now?"
```

**Document:** Industry, revenue, ad spend, team size, results

#### Identifying Pain Points (10 minutes)
```
1. "What prompted you to look for an agency now?"
2. "What's your biggest challenge with your current ads?"
3. "How is your CAC trending over the past 3-6 months?"
4. "What would doubling your ROAS mean for your business?"
5. "Have you worked with an agency before? What worked/didn't work?"
```

**Document:** Pain level (1-10), specific problems, past experiences

#### Budget & Decision Process (7 minutes)
```
1. "What would you need to see to invest in an agency?"
2. "What's your budget for agency fees?"
3. "Who else is involved in this decision?"
4. "What's your timeline for making a decision?"
5. "If we could reduce your CAC by 30%, what would that be worth to you?"
```

**Document:** Budget range, decision-makers, timeline, buying criteria

#### Closing (3 minutes)
```
"Based on what you've shared, I think we could definitely help you
reach your goals. Here's what I'd recommend:

1. Schedule a strategy session where we'll audit your current ads
2. Create a custom growth plan for your business
3. If it makes sense, we'll discuss next steps

How does that sound?"
```

**IF YES:** Book strategy session
**IF HESITANT:** "What concerns do you have?"
**IF NO:** Thank them for their time

## Qualification Checklist

### Before the Call
- [ ] Review their website and LinkedIn
- [ ] Check company revenue (if public)
- [ ] Look at their current ads (if visible)
- [ ] Prepare 3 relevant case studies
- [ ] Have pricing ready

### During the Call
- [ ] Ask all qualification questions
- [ ] Document answers in CRM
- [ ] Calculate score within 5 minutes of call
- [ ] Assign status (Priority/Qualified/Nurture/Disqualify)
- [ ] Schedule next step if qualified

### After the Call
- [ ] Send thank you email within 2 hours
- [ ] Send promised resources (case studies, etc.)
- [ ] Add to appropriate nurture sequence
- [ ] Set follow-up reminders
- [ ] Update CRM with all notes

## Common Disqualification Reasons

### Budget Issues
- Ad spend too low (<PHP 50k/month)
- Only wants performance-based pricing
- Expects agency to work for "exposure"

### Authority Issues
- Talking to someone without budget authority
- Too many decision-makers (analysis paralysis)
- Founder keeps changing mind

### Need Issues
- No clear pain point
- Just "exploring options"
- Happy with current results

### Timeline Issues
- No urgency (just researching)
- Waiting for next year's budget
- Want to wait until after busy season

### Fit Issues
- Wrong industry (not e-commerce/SaaS)
- Too small (<PHP 10M revenue)
- Outside geographic area
- Internal team already handles ads well

## Conversion Benchmarks

### Target Metrics
- **Leads to Qualified:** 40-50%
- **Qualified to Discovery Call:** 60-70%
- **Discovery Call to Proposal:** 50-60%
- **Proposal to Close:** 60-80%

### If You're Missing Targets
- **<40% leads qualified:** Improve targeting/qualification criteria
- **<60% qualified to calls:** Strengthen value proposition
- **<50% calls to proposals:** Improve discovery process
- **<60% proposals to close:** Adjust pricing or add incentives

## Red Flags (Immediate Disqualification)

### Budget Red Flags
- Asked about "discounts" in first email
- Comparing only on price
- Budget significantly below market rate
- Expects you to work for free initially

### Authority Red Flags
- "My boss needs to approve" (no access to boss)
- "We're still evaluating" (for 6+ months)
- Decision-maker keeps changing
- Won't commit to a call

### Need Red Flags
- "Just looking" with no timeline
- Happy with current results
- No specific goals
- Wants you to "prove yourself" with free work

### Fit Red Flags
- Wrong industry (not e-commerce/SaaS)
- Too small or too large
- Internal team already excellent
- Competitor (another agency)
