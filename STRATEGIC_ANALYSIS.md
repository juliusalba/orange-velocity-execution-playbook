## Execution Roadmap Refinements

### Month 1: Foundation (CRITICAL)

**Priority 1: Get 3-5 Clients FAST**
- Don't spend 2 weeks on legal/branding
- Start with DBA or simple registration
- Use Canva for logo (1 day)
- Build landing page on Carrd or Framer (2-3 days)

**Priority 2: Document AFTER First Client**
- Don't create SOPs before serving clients
- Document process with real examples
- Refine as you go

**Priority 3: Hire Strategic**
- Don't hire a generalist
- Hire for your weakness (likely creative or operations)
- Trial period: 1 month, then evaluate

### Month 2: Operations (CRITICAL)

**Focus: Systems Before Scaling**
- Build dashboard BEFORE taking on 10+ clients
- Set up automation to save 10 hours/week
- Document everything for team training

**Client Acquisition:** 5-8 new clients
- Focus on similar businesses to Month 1 clients
- Use Month 1 clients for referrals
- Keep CAC (customer acquisition cost) < PHP 5k

### Month 3: Authority (OPPORTUNITY)

**This is your inflection point**
- Shift from founder-dependent to system-dependent
- Build content that generates inbound leads
- Get 5 video testimonials (not just written)

**Key Metric:** 50% of clients from inbound/referrals by end of Month 3

### Month 4-6: Scale (EXECUTION)

**At this point, you should have:**
- 20-30+ clients
- 4-6 person team
- PHP 350k+ MRR
- Systems that run without you

**Focus:** Optimize, don't just add clients
- Raise prices 10-15%
- Fire bottom 10% clients
- Focus on enterprise tier

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Revenue Metrics
- **MRR:** Track monthly (goal: PHP 500k by Month 6)
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User):** PHP 25-30k/month
- **Client Churn Rate:** <10% monthly
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** <PHP 8k per client
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** >3:1

### Operational Metrics
- **Team Utilization:** 80-90% (not 100%, leave buffer)
- **Client Satisfaction:** >90% (measured by NPS or surveys)
- **Project Completion Rate:** 95% on time
- **Time to First Value:** <30 days for new clients

### Growth Metrics
- **Pipeline:** Always have 2x next month's target in pipeline
- **Conversion Rate:** 20% of demos convert to clients
- **Referral Rate:** 30% of new clients from referrals
- **Inbound vs Outbound Ratio:** 50/50 by Month 4

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. **Add "Buffer Months"**
Plan for 7-8 months, not 6. Account for:
- Holidays (December)
- Market fluctuations
- Learning curves
- Unexpected challenges

### 2. **Define Ideal Client Profile (ICP)**

**Primary ICP:**
- E-commerce business
- Revenue: PHP 5-20M annually
- Currently spending PHP 100k+ on ads
- Has tried agencies before (frustrated)
- Decision maker: Founder or CMO

**Secondary ICP:**
- SaaS business
- Series A+ funded
- Growth-focused
- Willing to pay premium for results

**Avoid:**
- Businesses with <PHP 1M revenue
- Price-sensitive clients
- Industries with ad restrictions (crypto, adult, etc.)

2. **Scale:** We take you from 6-figure to 7-figure revenue
3. **Systems:** Proprietary process (your "secret sauce")

### 4. **Build a "Case Study Factory"**

## Risk Analysis

### High-Risk Scenarios

**1. Client Churn Crisis**
- *Risk:* 30%+ churn in Month 3-4
- *Mitigation:* Build retention into contracts, provide exceptional service
- *Backup Plan:* Pipeline 2x target, lower prices temporarily to retain

**2. Team Scaling Issues**
- *Risk:* Can't hire fast enough or hire wrong people
- *Mitigation:* Use trial periods, detailed job descriptions
- *Backup Plan:* Contractor model before full-time hires

**3. Market Saturation**
- *Risk:* Run out of prospects in Philippines
- *Mitigation:* Expand to Malaysia/Singapore by Month 6
- *Backup Plan:* Vertical specialization (SaaS only)

**4. Platform Changes**
- *Risk:* iOS updates, algorithm changes
- *Mitigation:* Diversify across Facebook, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn
- *Backup Plan:* Pivot to organic/influencer marketing

**5. Founder Burnout**
- *Risk:* Working 80+ hour weeks
- *Mitigation:* Hire ops manager by Month 4
- *Backup Plan:* Reduce client load, raise prices
