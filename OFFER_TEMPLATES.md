## Overview: 3-Tier Pricing Structure

**Starter Tier** (PHP 15-20k/month)
- New businesses or testing agency partnership
- Core deliverables only
- Good for proof of concept

**Growth Tier** (PHP 25-35k/month) - MOST POPULAR
- Established businesses ready to scale
- Full service with optimization
- 70% of clients will choose this

**Enterprise Tier** (PHP 50-75k/month)
- Large businesses or urgent needs
- Premium support + additional services
- Highest margins

# PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

## Why This Pricing Works

### Anchor Pricing
- Show Enterprise tier first (PHP 75k)
- Growth tier seems reasonable at PHP 28k
- Starter tier feels like a steal at PHP 15k

### Value-Based Pricing
- Price based on results, not time
- ROI calculation: "If we increase your revenue by PHP 500k/month, PHP 28k is 5.6%"

### Good-Better-Best
- **Starter:** Good (basic needs)
- **Growth:** Better (most popular)
- **Enterprise:** Best (comprehensive)

### Scarcity
- "We only take 10 clients per month"
- "Enterprise tier has 3 spots available"
- "Growth tier waitlist is 2 weeks"

### Risk Reversal
- Guarantees at every tier
- Shorter contracts for Starter
- Proof of concept approach

# OBJECTION HANDLING

## "Your price is too high"

### Response Strategy
```
"I appreciate you being upfront about that. Help me understand - are you comparing us to other agencies, or is it just not in your budget right now?

[IF BUDGET]: What budget were you working with?
[IF COMPARISON]: Here's what makes us different: [list value props]

Let me show you the math:
Current: You're spending PHP XXX,XXX/month on ads with X.Xx ROAS
After: You'll spend PHP XXX,XXX/month with X.Xx ROAS
Impact: PHP XXX,XXX additional revenue per month
Our Fee: PHP XX,XXX/month
Net Impact: PHP XXX,XXX additional profit

Price is what you pay, value is what you get. In this case, you're getting XX% ROI on our investment.

Does that help put it in perspective?"
```

## "We're happy with our current results"

### Response Strategy
```
"That's great to hear! I'm curious though - what would taking your current results to the next level mean for your business?

- Reduce your CPA by 30%
```
